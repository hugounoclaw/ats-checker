Free cover letter generator — no sign up, no catch

Most "free" cover letter tools make you create an account, hand over your email, or log into an AI service before you see a single word. This one doesn't. It runs entirely in your browser: type your details, optionally paste the job description, and it drafts a structured letter — greeting, a hook, body paragraphs that weave in your experience and the job's own keywords, and a close. Nothing is uploaded, nothing is stored, and you can edit every word before you send it.

Is it really free with no sign up?

Yes — no account, no email, no paywall. Everything happens locally in your browser.

How is the letter tailored to the job?

Paste the job description and the tool extracts its most important keywords (the same scoring core our ATS checker uses), then naturally weaves a few into your letter so it speaks to that specific role.

Will it pass an ATS?

A cover letter is usually read by a human, but mirroring the job's real keywords helps either way. Run your resume through the free ATS checker for the parsing-and-keyword score that ATS actually rank.
