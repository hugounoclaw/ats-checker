Tax Accountant ATS Resume Checklist (2026)

12 ATS-resume checks Tax Accountants need to pass in 2026, the keywords recruiters scan for, and three role-specific resume bullets to copy.

Tax resumes are filtered by form numbers, tax software, and CPA or EA credential status. Recruiters at CPA firms and corporate tax departments search for '1040, 1120, 1120-S, 1065,' specific software ('CCH Axcess, UltraTax, Lacerte, ProSystem fx, OneSource, Corptax'), and credential acronyms (CPA, EA, JD-LLM Tax, MST). Generic 'prepared tax returns' wording without form numbers and software is a fast rejection.

Mid-level seekers should quantify return volume, complexity (multi-state, consolidated, international), and any specialty areas (R&D credit under §174, transfer pricing, sales/use tax via Avalara or Vertex, partnership allocations). Senior reviewers want IRS-notice resolution, audit-defense, and ASC 740 provision experience. Public-firm candidates need to show busy-season hours and chargeable utilization.

The 12-point ATS checklist for Tax Accountants

  1. Declare CPA, EA, or JD-LLM with stateWrite 'CPA, New York #098765, active' or 'Enrolled Agent, EA #00123456' or 'JD/LLM Taxation, NYU 2019, NY Bar admitted'. Tax recruiters filter by credential type then by jurisdiction.
  2. List tax software with module and versionSpecify 'CCH Axcess Tax (1040, 1120, 1065 modules), UltraTax CS 2023, Lacerte, ProSystem fx, GoSystem RS, OneSource Income Tax, Corptax'. Software match is a tight filter at every firm.
  3. Name return forms prepared by volumeQuote 'prepared 220 Form 1040s, 45 Form 1120-S, 38 Form 1065 with 14 multi-state allocations, 12 Form 1120 consolidated' rather than 'prepared various returns'. Form codes plus volumes rank candidates instantly.
  4. Show multi-state and apportionment workSpecify 'prepared apportionment workpapers for 27 states, handled NY/CA/TX/IL combined filings, throwback rule analysis' to prove SALT depth beyond federal-only practice.
  5. Cite IRC sections and key tax provisionsReference '§174 R&D capitalization, §163(j) interest limitation, §199A QBI, §168(k) bonus depreciation, §382 NOL limitation' to signal real code fluency, not just form prep.
  6. List ASC 740 provision experienceMention 'prepared quarterly and annual ASC 740 income tax provision, deferred tax rollforward, UTP analysis per ASC 740-10, prepared FIN 48 schedule'. Corporate tax filters on ASC 740 heavily.
  7. Show sales/use tax toolsNote 'Avalara AvaTax integration with NetSuite across 35 nexus states, Vertex O Series, monthly filings via Sovos' since indirect tax is a distinct hiring track.
  8. Include IRS and state-notice resolutionSpecify 'resolved 14 IRS CP2000 notices in 2024, 6 state-tax audits, drafted Form 843 abatement requests' to prove controversy capability beyond preparation.
  9. Quantify busy-season hours and chargeable utilizationQuote 'averaged 62 chargeable hours/week Jan-Apr, 84% utilization YTD, 1,820 chargeable hours FY2024' since public-firm recruiters use these as performance proxies.
  10. Show R&D credit, transfer pricing, or international tax if anyList '§41 R&D credit studies, transfer pricing documentation per §482 and OECD BEPS Action 13, Form 5471 and 8865 for CFCs' since specialty practices command premium hires.
  11. Include depreciation tools and fixed-asset workMention 'Sage Fixed Assets, BNA Fixed Assets, depreciation methods (MACRS, §168(k), §179)' to prove FA cycle ownership beyond the trial balance.
  12. Name research databasesList 'CCH IntelliConnect, Checkpoint, Bloomberg Tax, Tax Analysts; drafted 8 memos with primary-source citations' since research authorship distinguishes senior tax staff.

FAQ — Tax Accountant ATS questions

Should I list 'CPA Candidate' on my tax resume?

Yes if you have passed at least one section. Format as 'CPA Candidate: REG passed July 2024 (score 84), AUD scheduled Jan 2025.' For tax-only roles where EA is acceptable, also mention EA status if you have it. Public-firm recruiters reward visible exam progress and date specificity.

How do I show busy-season throughput without naming clients?

Quote 'prepared 180 individual returns and 42 entity returns Jan 15 to Apr 15, 1,840 chargeable hours FY2024 at 86% utilization, zero filed extensions due to preparer delay.' Hours, return counts, and utilization are firm metrics, not client-identifying details.

Is ASC 740 experience required for corporate tax roles?

Yes for any public company or PE-backed business preparing audited financials. Even mid-sized private companies often run provisions to support GAAP reporting. If you have not run a provision yet, mention exposure to it (PBC support, rate reconciliation review) and pair with the CPA AUD or FAR pass to signal readiness.

Do I list state tax software separately from federal?

List the package once if it covers both (CCH Axcess, UltraTax, ProSystem fx). For specialty SALT roles, separately list Vertex, Avalara, Sovos, or TaxJar for indirect tax; ONESOURCE or Corptax for income tax. Indirect-tax recruiters filter on Avalara/Vertex specifically.

How do I describe IRS notice work without revealing client info?

Use the notice type and outcome: 'resolved CP2000 notice on $48K underreported 1099-MISC, drafted response with substantiation, achieved full abatement.' Notice codes (CP2000, CP504, LT11, Form 1058) are standard IRS identifiers and contain no client information.
