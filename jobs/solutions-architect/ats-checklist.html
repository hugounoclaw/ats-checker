Solutions Architect ATS Resume Checklist (2026)

12 ATS-resume checks Solutions Architects need to pass in 2026, the keywords recruiters scan for, and three role-specific resume bullets to copy.

Solutions Architect is one of the few engineering-adjacent roles where presentation and customer-facing language matter as much as technical depth. ATS systems scan for both the technical stack and the pre-sales vocabulary: RFP, PoC, reference architecture, customer workshops, statement of work. Missing the pre-sales layer puts your resume in the developer pile instead of the SA pile.

In 2026 most SA roles sit inside cloud vendors, ISVs, or large consultancies and the role spans technical design, customer enablement, and revenue influence. Hiring managers look for evidence you have closed deals, unblocked PoCs, and produced architecture artifacts that customers actually used, not just slideware.

The 12-point ATS checklist for Solutions Architects

  1. Show customer-facing scope, not just internal designPhrase it as led architecture workshops with 14 enterprise customers, not designed systems. SA hiring managers screen for comfort in front of customer teams as much as for technical skill.
  2. Quote influenced or sourced revenueInfluenced 4.2M ARR across 9 closed deals by leading technical validation. Revenue numbers are core SA currency and missing them implies you operate as a pure engineer.
  3. Name a reference framework: AWS WAR, Azure WAF, TOGAFAWS Well-Architected Framework, Azure Well-Architected, Google Cloud Architecture Framework, or TOGAF 10. Vendor-specific frameworks are stronger at vendor and partner shops; TOGAF still helps at large enterprises.
  4. List the diagramming tool and a deliverable countAuthored 38 reference architectures in Lucidchart, draw.io, or Excalidraw. SA work is artifact-driven and a deliverable count proves you ship, not just whiteboard.
  5. Show RFP or RFI response experienceLed technical sections on 11 RFP responses, win rate 64 percent. RFP experience is gold for enterprise SA roles and most candidates underweight it on the resume.
  6. Mention multi-cloud or hybrid only if realTrue multi-cloud SAs are rare and valuable. If you only ran one cloud, say so. Overclaiming multi-cloud is the most common interview red flag for SA candidates.
  7. Add Proof-of-Concept (PoC) leadership with outcomesLed 23 customer PoCs with 78 percent conversion to paid. PoC ownership is a specific SA workflow distinct from internal engineering and reviewers want the conversion number.
  8. Show industry vertical depth where you have itHealthcare (HIPAA), financial services (PCI DSS, SOC 2), public sector (FedRAMP), retail. Vertical-specific SAs command higher salary and screen better at vendor partner orgs.
  9. List partnership or alliance experienceWorked with AWS PSA team on 4 joint accounts, ran 2 Immersion Days. Alliance work signals you can navigate co-sell motions, which most SA roles require.
  10. Include certifications across two clouds if you have themAWS SA Professional plus Azure AZ-305, or GCP Professional Cloud Architect plus AWS SA Associate. Cross-cloud certs unlock SA roles at consulting firms specifically.
  11. Show one cost or licensing modeling bulletBuilt TCO model showing 38 percent 3-year savings vs incumbent vendor, used in final-round procurement. Cost modeling is a quiet SA superpower that closes deals.
  12. Quote a customer-quotable win or named referenceCustomer case study published on vendor blog, or named reference for 6 prospect calls. Named-reference customers are a strong SA signal and most candidates fail to surface them.

Common ATS rejection reasons for Solutions Architects

✗ Reads as senior engineer with no customer-facing language
Fix:Add workshops led, PoCs run, RFPs responded to, and customer-meeting counts; SA hiring managers screen on this first.
✗ No revenue, deal size, or pipeline influence mentioned
Fix:Add an influenced ARR figure or deal-count number; this is the headline metric for vendor and partner SA roles.
✗ Claims multi-cloud but only one cloud appears in bullets
Fix:Demote the others to certifications or a single Familiar line, and lead with the cloud you have deep production scope in.
✗ Lists 20+ technologies as a flat keyword wall
Fix:Cluster around 3-5 reference architectures you owned end to end; SAs are judged on synthesis, not tool breadth.
✗ No certifications listed for a vendor-aligned SA role
Fix:Add the relevant vendor Professional-level cert (or current Associate) with date; vendor SA roles often require it as a hiring floor.

FAQ — Solutions Architect ATS questions

Do I need TOGAF certification to apply for Solutions Architect roles?

Only at large traditional enterprises and consulting firms (Accenture, Deloitte, Capgemini) where enterprise architecture maturity is high. At cloud vendors, ISVs, and most tech companies, a vendor Professional cert plus shipped reference architectures matters more than TOGAF.

How do I show revenue influence if I am not in sales?

SAs are pre-sales technical owners and revenue attribution is normal. Pull the deal sizes from Salesforce or ask your AE. Influenced ARR, won deals where you led technical evaluation, and PoC conversion rate are all fair and expected.

Should I list every architecture diagram I have ever drawn?

No. Pick 3-5 reference patterns you owned end to end (multi-region web tier, event-driven data platform, hybrid identity, etc.) and treat them as anchors. Quality and reuse beats volume for SA hiring.

Is post-sales architect experience valuable for pre-sales roles?

Yes, increasingly. Pre-sales hiring managers value architects who have lived with their own designs in production because they propose more realistic solutions. Surface it explicitly: spent 18 months in post-sales delivery for designs I proposed.

How important is the customer-presenting portion of the interview?

Critical. Most SA loops include a whiteboard or slide presentation to a mock customer. Recruiters screen the resume for evidence you can do this: workshops led, conferences spoken at, customer training delivered. Add those bullets if they exist.
